# Product Guide

A guide to Transcribble, a workspace for bringing recordings or media in, reviewing transcript text, organizing work, and exporting useful results.

## Main Uses

- Recording and import paths
- Transcript review
- Workspace organization
- Export paths

## Typical Flow

1. Open the live Transcribble site.
2. Choose the launch or workspace path.
3. Bring material in, review the transcript, organize the recording, and export the useful result.
